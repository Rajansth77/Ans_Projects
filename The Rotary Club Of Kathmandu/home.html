A Message From The President 2017-18

Jo Logghe


This year Rotary International embraces the theme ‘Be the Inspiration’. How can we fulfill this? How can we be the inspiration? It is only by uniting as a family that we can inspire others to do the same. Let’s let our fellowship help us to become “People of Action”. This is what ROTARY means to me. So this year, we will work to make actions speak louder than words: let our words teach and our actions speak. Too often we are full of words but empty of action. Let us join hands to work together and in so doing, be the inspiration for a lot of people. For now, no more words, only a big thank you for you trust in me.

Rotary Club of Kathmandu Midtown
