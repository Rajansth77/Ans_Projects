Promoted by ANS Co, Stuti Mero Mart Pvt. Ltd is a chain of convenience stores at a number of major neighbourhoods in Kathmandu. at Ghattekulo, Katyayani Chowk, Pepsi Cola, Sinamangal and Kapan. Providing a one-stop shopping solution, Stuti Mero Mart is a preferable address for consumers to make all their purchases at one place. The outlets are at locations where the population is high with the aim of cashing in on changing consumer behavior targeting shoppers who are used to visiting different shops to buy daily items.
